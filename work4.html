Endlessly:

Through captivating rainbow hues symbolizing dopamine-inducing content, this art exhibition critiques the alluring yet hollow nature of dopamine addiction. It raises awareness of the pervasive tendency to succumb to addictive content while simultaneously experiencing a sense of emptiness. With the aim to provoke introspection, the exhibition serves as a wake-up call to the dangers of this addiction phenomenon.



Never-Ending

"Endlessly: Never-Ending" is an immersive artwork that explores the addictive nature of scrolling digital contents. Through captivating cellphone lights illuminating a face, the artwork prompts defamiliarization, encouraging viewers to reflect on their own digital behaviors and adopt a more mindful approach to technology usage. Interaction with the artwork mirrors mindless swiping on social media, fostering introspection and a renewed perspective.



Never-Filled

"Endlessly: Never-Filled" explores the addictive nature of dopamine-inducing content. Inspired by social media platforms' manipulation tactics, the artwork represents a slot machine interface. Pulling the lever reveals unpredictable and colorful content, enticing viewers to continuously engage. The vibrant shifting rectangles symbolize the disorientation and emptiness experienced after excessive content consumption. The title emphasizes the futility of filling oneself with dopamine, illustrating the insatiable nature of digital consumption. Reflect on your actions as you pull the lever endlessly.



Catch Dopamine!

"Endlesssly: Catch Dopamine!" highlights the elusive nature of dopamine-seeking behaviors. In this interactive artwork, viewers move a box to catch dopamine symbols, symbolizing the relentless pursuit of dopamine-inducing content. The box represents the viewer's quest for gratification, often leading to a state of confusion and decision fatigue. As the box fills, a larger box emerges, signifying the ever-expanding threshold for satisfaction. Ultimately, the artwork challenges the notion of reaching a limit and portrays the perpetual pursuit of dopamine.
